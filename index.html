Food Menu

PIZZA

Savor our artisanal pizzas crafted with fresh ingredients and baked to perfection in a traditional wood-fired oven. Each slice offers a symphony of flavors, from savory meats to vibrant vegetables, ensuring a delightful culinary experience at every bite.

Price: ₹ 200

COLD COFFEE

Indulge in our refreshing cold coffee, meticulously brewed to deliver a smooth blend of rich espresso and chilled milk. Perfectly balanced and served over ice, it's a revitalizing treat that embodies pure coffee bliss for every palate.

Price: ₹ 99

Pav Bhaji

Savor our signature pav bhaji, a flavorful Mumbai street food delicacy. Mashed vegetables simmered in aromatic spices, served with buttered buns. A beloved dish bursting with authentic Indian flavors, crafted to delight every palate.

Price: ₹ 80

DAL CHAWAL

Enjoy our comforting dal chawal, a traditional Indian staple. Fragrant basmati rice paired with creamy lentils simmered in spices, served with a dollop of ghee. A wholesome and nourishing dish that embodies homemade goodness and authentic flavors.

Price: ₹ 90

DESSERTS

Indulge in our exquisite dessert: a rich, molten chocolate lava cake served warm with a velvety scoop of vanilla bean ice cream, drizzled with a decadent raspberry coulis. Perfectly balanced textures and flavors await, promising a sweet finale to your dining experience at our esteemed restaurant.

Price: ₹ 180

SALAD

Enjoy our vibrant garden salad, a delightful medley of crisp mixed greens, heirloom tomatoes, cucumber ribbons, and shaved radishes, tossed in a zesty lemon vinaigrette. Topped with toasted pine nuts and crumbled feta cheese, this refreshing salad is a testament to freshness and flavor, perfect for a light and wholesome meal.

Price: ₹ 75
